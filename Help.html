How long do withdrawals take?

A 12-hour pending period is applicable to your first withdrawal, all withdrawals after that will be credited to your preferred payment method within 24 hours. Withdrawals are processed Monday-Friday (EFT) and Monday-Saturday (eWallet).

I forgot my password.

You can click on forgot password, then you will be able to set a new one through a link that will be sent to you on your email. Or you can login using gmail, twitter or facebook.

Are there any fees for withdrawal?

No! We don't charge any withdrawal fees. However, your payment provider may charge a fee to receive a payment from us, so if you're concerned, check with them first about whether a fee will or will not be applied.

Why was my bet cancelled?

If your bet has been cancelled, your stake will be fully returned to you. Bet cancellations happen because the market was offered with incorrect information and a technical error occurred on our side

Why can't I log in?

You've entered the wrong password, There are technical issues with our website, Your account has been closed or Your account may have been blocked
